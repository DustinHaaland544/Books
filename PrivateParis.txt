Private Paris" is a novel by James Patterson, published in 2016. The book is a part of James Patterson Private series, which follows the story of Jack Morgan, the head of
the world's most elite detective agency, Private. In this book, Jack is called to Paris to investigate the kidnapping of a famous French businessman's daughter. The 
businessman, who is also an art collector, is willing to pay any amount to get his daughter back and Jack is the only one he trusts to handle the case.

As Jack delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of lies and corruption that threatens to engulf him. He finds out that the kidnappers are not just after the ransom
money, but also the priceless art collection that the businessman possess. Jack must navigate through the dangerous streets of Paris, where he is constantly under 
surveillance, and outsmart the kidnappers who are always one step ahead.

Jack is not alone in this case, he has his team to help him. He has his trusted friend and colleague, Justine Smith, who is in charge of Private's Paris office, and is 
also a valuable asset in this case. Together they work to uncover the truth behind the kidnappings, and the mysterious organization that seems to be behind it all.

As Jack races against time to find the kidnappers and bring them to justice, he must also confront his own past and the secrets that threaten to destroy his life. He must
come to terms with the loss of his wife, who was murdered in Paris years ago, and how it still haunts him. He must also confront his own guilt and the mistakes he made 
that led to her death.

"Private Paris" is a fast-paced thriller that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end. It is full of suspense, action and twists that will keep 
the reader guessing until the very end. The novel also has a romantic subplot, adding an emotional depth to the story. The setting of the novel, Paris, is beautifully 
described, and it adds to the overall atmosphere of the book. It is a perfect blend of mystery, crime and romance, making it a great read for anyone who loves a good 
thriller
